How to Request a Refund

1

Send an email to support@vaultcase.netlify.app with the subject line "Refund Request"

2

Include the email address associated with your VAULT account and the date of purchase

3

We will process your refund within 5-7 business days and confirm via email

After the 14-Day Period

Refund requests made after the 14-day window are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We may offer a partial refund or account credit at our discretion. Please contact us and explain your situation — we're here to help.

Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your account settings. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically trigger a refund for the current billing period. After cancellation, you retain Pro access until the end of the paid period.

All payments are processed by Paddle. Refunds are returned to the original payment method. Processing time depends on your bank or card issuer (typically 5–10 business days after we initiate the refund).
